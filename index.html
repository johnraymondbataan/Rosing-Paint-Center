About Rosing Paint Center

More than just a store we're your paint partners.

Founded in the heart of Atlanta, Rosing Paint Center has been serving the community for over 15 years. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted paint suppliers in the region.

We pride ourselves on our deep knowledge of paints and coatings, helping customers find exactly what they need for any project, big or small.

Visit us today at our Chamblee Tucker location and experience the difference that personal service and expertise can make.
